# Readme
This repo only consist of screenshots of my work during my contract with **WRK.House** in the development of the **Occurrence** app.

## App Description
> Occurrence is a in-situ human connections platform. Unlike traditional dating or friend meeting apps, Occurrence provides connections with real people in the real world. This removes many of the negatives of traditional connection apps. 

> Occurrence runs in the background of your everyday life and lets you know when there is someone you might be interested in that is in the same vicinity. Connections are made when both parties get to see each other in real life and they agree through the app that they want to meet.
